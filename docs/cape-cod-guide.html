🏖️ Sagamore, MA — Family Week Guide

July 5–12, 2026 · 18 people (14 adults, 4 kids) · 60 Old Plymouth Rd · House with pool

⏰ Book now — 2 weeks out
  • Lobster Trap / Chart Room / Fishermen's View — call this week for any group dinner. 18 is tough; 2 tables of 9 is easier
  • Water Wizz — buy tickets online ahead (~$40 GA / $38 kids) — 10am–7pm during your week
  • Heritage Museums & Gardens — $26 adult / $16 youth, buy online
  • Beach stickers — ask your rental host if Sandwich/Bourne stickers are included. Scusset is $14 MA / $40 out-of-state per car — or just walk, it's 0.7 mi
  • Sunday July 5 arrival — July 4th is Saturday, expect leftover bridge traffic. Arrive early morning or late evening

🚶 Walking distance

Sagamore Beach — 0.5 mi / 10 min. Neighborhood Cape Cod Bay beach. Check host for a Sandwich beach sticker.
Scusset Beach State Reservation ⭐ — 0.7 mi / 15 min. Walk and skip parking entirely. Lifeguards June 19–Labor Day, restrooms, showers, snack bar. If driving: $14 MA / $40 out-of-state, May 23–Oct 12.
Cape Cod Canal Path — 0.3 mi. 7-mile paved path, ship watching, bikes, strollers.
Sagamore Hill Trail — 0.6 mi. Easy 1.7-mi loop, canal views.
Canal fishing — 0.5 mi. Striped bass / bluefish in July.
Canal Crossways Inclusive Playground — 0.5 mi. Rubber surfaces, great for toddlers.

🎯 Things to do

  • Cape Cod Canal Bike Path — free, all ages
  • Canal Fishing — striped bass / bluefish
  • Cape Cod Canal Visitor Center — free, near Scusset Beach, kids love the boat/radar displays
  • Heritage Museums & Gardens — Sandwich, 100 acres, car collection, carousel. 10am–5pm daily. $26 / $16

  • Aptucxet Trading Post — Bourne, oldest trading post in America (1627)

🦞 Restaurants — lobster rolls

The Lobster Trap — 290 Shore Rd, Bourne (~15 min). Since 1969. Fish market attached. Full menu + non-seafood options. Open 11am–9/10pm daily. Call ahead for 18.
Fishermen's View — 20 Freezer Rd, Sandwich Marina (~10 min). Two fishermen brothers, waterfront. Lobster roll on Texas toast. Raw bar, sushi. Make a reservation.
Seafood Shanty — 803 Scenic Hwy, Buzzards Bay (~10 min). Roadside shack, "best lobster roll on Cape Cod" per Tripadvisor. Outdoor only, cash-friendly.
East Wind Lobster & Grille — Bourne Bridge side (~10 min). Three lobster roll styles, live lobster market. Buy fresh seafood to cook at the house.
Chart Room — Cataumet Marina (~20 min). Converted railroad barge on the harbor, Portuguese bread lobster roll. Seasonal — late June through Labor Day. Reservations a must.

Other good spots

  • Whaleback Restaurant — 1052 Sandwich Rd, Sagamore. Right in your neighborhood. Burgers, seafood, pizza. Affordable.
  • The Sagamore — 1131 Sandwich Rd. Family-owned, outdoor Sperry tent, good for a group night.
  • Cafe Chew — Merchants Square, Sandwich. Breakfast & lunch, 8am–3pm.
  • Beth's Bakery & Cafe — 16 Jarves St, Sandwich. Quiche, scones, pastries.
  • Stir Crazy — 570 MacArthur Blvd, Buzzards Bay. Cambodian, 25+ years. Great veg/pescatarian options.
  • Mezza Luna — Main St, Buzzards Bay. Best sit-down Italian in the area.
  • Grill & More — 340 Main St, Buzzards Bay. Lebanese/Mediterranean — shawarma, falafel, hummus. Plenty of vegetarian & dairy-free. Your sister and brother-in-law will love this.
  • Treehouse Brewing — Sandwich. Craft beer + pizza, food trucks.

🍕 Pizza night — ordering in

  • Luzia's Pizza Napoletana — Neapolitan, light airy charred crust
  • Trading Post Lounge — South Shore bar pizza, ask for it "laced"
  • Buzzards Bay House of Pizza — Greek-style, delivers
  • Ella's Wood Burning Oven — wood-fired, a step up

Dining out tip: call ahead everywhere. 18 is impossible walk-in. Split into 2×9 or 3×6. Grab-and-go (Seafood Shanty, East Wind) is your friend.

🍦 Ice cream

  • Twin Acres Ice Cream Shoppe — 21 Route 6A, Sandwich, ~5 min. 70+ flavors, vegan / sorbet / no-sugar-added
  • The Shipwreck Ice Cream — 2 Freezer Rd, Sandwich, ~5 min. Pirate-themed, big yard
  • Betty Ann's Dairy Freeze — 225 Main St, Buzzards Bay, ~10 min. Soft serve legend. Cash only
  • Seaside Scoops & Grille — 100 Main St, Bourne, ~10 min. Ice cream + food
  • Smitty's Homemade — Sandwich/Forestdale, ~10 min
  • Four Seas Ice Cream ⭐ — 360 S Main St, Centerville, ~25 min. Open since 1934. National Geographic best-in-the-world. The pilgrimage.

Go after 7:30pm when lines die down. Grab pints to take back to the house. Twin Acres has your dairy-free options covered.

🍽️ Meal plan — 7 nights

Dietary lineup: Dairy-free (1) · Gluten-free (1) · Vegetarian (couple) · Pescatarian (1) · Omnivores (rest)

Night 1 — Taco Bar 🌮
Fish / black beans / beef. Corn tortillas (GF). Cashew-lime crema for dairy-free.
Night 2 — Grilled Salmon & Veggie 🐟
Salmon + portobello "steaks". Corn, rosemary potatoes, green salad. All naturally GF.
Night 3 — Burger Night 🍔
Beef, turkey, black bean, salmon burgers. GF buns + lettuce wraps. Oven fries.
Night 4 — Shrimp Boil / Clambake 🦐
Shrimp, clams, mussels, corn, potatoes. Newspaper table. Veggie pasta on the side.
Night 5 — Mediterranean 🫒
Za'atar chicken, swordfish/shrimp skewers, stuffed peppers. Hummus, baba ganoush, quinoa tabbouleh (GF), Greek salad.
Night 6 — Pasta Night 🍝
Regular + GF penne. Marinara, dairy-free pesto, aglio e olio. Shrimp / sausage / meatballs on the side.
Night 7 — Pizza Night 🍕
DIY pizza station — regular + GF crusts, dairy-free cheese option. Kids love making their own.
